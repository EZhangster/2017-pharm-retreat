Poster Session Info

Overview

The Dr. Louise M. Nutter Memorial Poster Session will be held at this year's annual retreat from 1:00 pm - 2:30 pm on September 15 in the east wing 2nd floor of the Commons Hotel.

Posters will be judged by an independent panel of faculty, postdocs, and graduate students in three separate categories: Graduate Students, Postdocs, and Undergrads/Post-Bacs.

A certificate and gift card will be awarded to posters with the best scores in each category.

Register

Please register and submit your poster to Jim Shoemaker (shoem054@umn.edu) by August 15, 2017.

If your poster information will not be ready by August 15th, please e-mail Jim for details.

Register Your Poster >>

Additional Information

  • Poster Easels will be available as first come, first serve starting from 7:30 am at the Commons Hotel.
  • Posters boards will be standard 40" x 60" in size.
  • Awards will be given in the following categories:
    • Postdoc Posters - 1 Winner
    • Graduate Posters - 1 Winner, 1 Runner Up
    • Undergraduate/Postbac Posters - 1 Winner
  • If you have any questions or concerns or if you are unable to come in prior to the start of the retreat to set up your poster, please contact Jim Shoemaker (shoem054@umn.edu) or Eric Zhang (zhan0718@umn.edu)
